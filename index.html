About Me

Hey! I’m Zoë. A FullStack Web & Software developer who builds software that not only works but truly makes a difference. My journey in tech started from a simple curiosity and a genuine love for solving problems. Over time, I’ve come to see that every bug is a learning opportunity and every feature is a chance to create something beautiful.


I pour my heart into every project, whether I’m debugging a pesky issue or designing a feature that brings clarity and ease to users. I believe that technology should be as accessible and warm as it is powerful, blending creativity with functionality in all that I do.



Beyond the code, I'm a voracious reader with a soft spot for classic literature and an eclectic taste in music that keeps my creativity flowing. My evenings are often spent lost in a game, good book or discovering the next indie hit, which always inspires new ideas and keeps me grounded.



Join me on this journey, and let’s create something amazing together.
